SMS Notification Service

Our SMS notification service ("BOS") is an internal business operations tool that sends automated task reminders, daily briefings, and business notifications to the business owner. This service is not available to the general public.

Program Description

BOS (Builder Operating System) sends automated SMS messages including daily morning briefings, midday task reminders, weekly reviews, and confirmation replies to inbound messages. This is an internal business tool, not a marketing program.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Typical usage includes 2-3 automated messages per day (morning briefing, midday check) plus confirmation replies to inbound messages. Weekly review messages are sent once per week.

Opt-In and Opt-Out

The business owner opts in to the service by initiating the system and sending the first text message to the service number. To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. After opting out, no further messages will be sent. To resume, text START to the service number. For help, reply HELP.

Costs

There is no charge for the SMS service itself. However, standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply.

Supported Carriers

The service is compatible with major US wireless carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Carrier support may vary.
